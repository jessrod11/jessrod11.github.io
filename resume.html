Jessica 'Stix' Rodgers || Software Developer

My name is Jessica Rodgers, but most of my friends call me Stix. I am currently attending Nashville Software School as a Full Stack Software Developer Apprenctice. My story is a bit unique as I am attending this school for a second time. My first experience with coding was terrifying. I allowed that fear to get in the way of an amazing opportunity of learning and growing in front-end web development. After some time off, I thought I'd give it another chance and have completely fallen in love with writing code. Failure has never been an option and I achieve anything that I put my mind to. Once I made up my mind, the rest was history. So far, I've learned many technologies and skills that I've had the opportunity to apply to my portfolio. You can view some of those on my Portfolio page and/or my Github page.
